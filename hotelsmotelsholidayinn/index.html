We are getting married! August 16th, 2014 near Portland, OR

Hazel's list of Lodgings


Mount Hood:

  • The Huckleberry Inn, in the ski town of Government Camp, 10 minutes away from the ceremony location
  • Collins Lake Resort, hotel in Government Camp
  • Best Western Mt. Hood Inn, in Government Camp
  • The Lodge at Government Camp, condos in Government camp, a great place for a family or group

August is a wonderful time to camp at Mt. Hood, and we can provide you with tents, matts, and sleeping bags if you'd like to camp at Trillium Lake on Thursday and/or Friday night. Just let us know.

Portland:

  • The Kennedy School Hotel, 5 minutes away from the reception, in the NE Alberta Arts District
  • Caravan, the Tiny House Hotel in the Alberta Arts District, 4 minutes south of the reception
  • The White Eagle Saloon and Hotel, 8 minutes south
  • The Jupiter Hotel, 10 minutes south
  • The Red Lion on Hayden Island, 8 minutes north, on the Columbia River
  • The Georgian House Bed and Breakfast, 8 minutes south
  • Clinton St. Guesthouse, 20 minutes south

Portland is rather small, so any place you can find on the east or the west side will be within 20 minutes away from the venue. If you're looking for a more homey vibe, you can try the website for Air b & b. The Friendly Bike Guesthouse is a wonderful place if you hope to spend some time on a bicycle in the city. There are also a number of hostels and guest houses that have bunks and rooms available: Try the Northwest Portland Hostel or the Hawthorne Hostel.
